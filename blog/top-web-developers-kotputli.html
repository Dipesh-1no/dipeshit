3. Types of Websites Required for Different Businesses

Har business ki requirement alag hoti hai. Ek achha developer aapki jarurat ke hisab se website suggest karega:

  • Service/Business Websites: Ye doctors, lawyers, plumbers, ya consultants ke liye best hoti hai. Isme aapki services, portfolio, aur contact details hoti hain.
  • E-commerce Websites: Agar aap clothes, electronics, ya koi bhi product bechte hain, to E-commerce website aapko online orders lene aur payments receive karne ki suvidha deti hai.
  • Portfolio Websites: Photographers, freelancers, aur artists ke liye apni skills show karne ka best tarika.

Aaj ke time me sirf website banana kafi nahi hai, Automation aapke business ka asli hero hai. Automation ka matlab hai aise tools ka use karna jo aapka manual kaam kam kar dein aur efficiency badha dein.

Jo IT companies Website + App + Automation ka combo provide karti hain, wahi aapke business ko next level par le ja sakti hain.

7. Common Mistakes to Avoid While Hiring a Web Developer

Kotputli me website banwate waqt business owners aksar ye galtiyan karte hain:

  1. Saste ke Chakkar me Padhna: Kuch freelancers 2000-3000 rupaye me website banane ka wada karte hain, lekin wo free copy-paste templates use karte hain jisme na to security hoti hai aur na hi SEO. Aisi websites future me hack ho jati hain ya down rehti hain.
  2. Maintenance Support Check Na Karna: Website banne ke baad usme updates aur fixes ki zaroorat hoti hai. Agar aapka developer support nahi dega, to website kharab ho jayegi.
  3. No Focus on Mobile Design: 80% internet traffic mobile se aata hai. Agar developer sirf desktop design par focus kar raha hai, to ye ek red flag hai.
  4. Ignoring SEO completely: Bina SEO ke website ek aisi dukan hai jo jangal me khuli hai — waha koi customer nahi aayega.
