▶ Cyber Attacks Are Increasing – Click to Read

In today’s digital world, cyber attacks are growing every day. Hackers are trying to steal personal data, bank details, and company information using smart tricks.

🔍 Common Types of Cyber Attacks

  • Phishing: Fake emails or websites that steal your password.
  • Ransomware: A virus that locks your files and demands money.
  • Malware: Harmful software that damages your computer.

🛡️ How to Stay Safe from Cyber Attacks?

  • Use strong and different passwords for each account.
  • Turn on two-factor authentication (2FA).
  • Don’t click on unknown links in emails or messages.
  • Install antivirus software and keep it updated.
  • Update your apps and system regularly.
  • Don’t share personal information on unknown websites.

Cyber attacks can be dangerous, but with small steps and awareness, you can protect yourself and your data easily.
