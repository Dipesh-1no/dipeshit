Shivdansinghpura (Nidar Dham): Aastha, Sanskriti aur Shiksha ka Sangam

Rajasthan ke Kotputli–Behror region me sthit Shivdansinghpura gaav apni dharmik aastha, samajik ekta aur shiksha ke liye jaana jata hai.
Ye gaav Behror se lagbhag 17 kilometer ki doori par, Aravali ki pahadiyon ke beech basa hua hai. Local log ise Sai Mata ke mele aur Nidar Dham ke naam se bhi pehchante hain.

Gaav ki Sthapna aur Itihaas

Varsh 1922 me Raja Jaswant Singh ke mantri dwara apne purvajon ke naam par Shivdansinghpura gaav ki sthapna ki gayi thi.
Lagbhag 1800 ki aabadi wala ye gaav apni do vishesh pehchaanon ke liye mashhoor hai:

  • Sai Mata ka Mela
  • Nidar Dham Mandir

Shiksha aur Sarkari Sevaon me Gaav ki Bhagidari

Shivdansinghpura ki saksharta dar lagbhag 90% hai, jo ise ek progressive gaav banati hai. Yahan ke yuvaon me sarkari naukri ko lekar kaafi jagrukta dekhi jati hai.

    Gaav se:
  • RAS Officer
  • Rajpatrit

par niyukt vyaktiyon ka hona, poore gaav ke liye garv ki baat hai.

Sai Mata ka Mela: Shraddha aur Utsav ka Mahaparv

Sai Mata ka mela Shivdansinghpura ka sabse bada aur prasiddh aayojan hai. Ye mela do din tak chalta hai, jisme poora gaav milkar vyavastha sambhalta hai.

Pehla Din: Khel Pratiyogitaayein

  • Running (Daud)
  • Rassa-kassi
  • Long Jump
  • High Jump
  • Volleyball

Doosra Din: Kushti Pratiyogita

Kushti mukabla iska sabse bada attraction hota hai. Inaami rashi ₹1,00,000 rakhi gayi hai.

Raat ka Karyakram: Ragni Competition

Mele ki raat Ragni Competition ka aayojan hota hai, jiska uddeshya lok-sanskriti aur paramparaon ko zinda rakhna hai.

Nidar Dham aur Baba Chetan Das Ji ka Aadhyatmik Mahatva

Nidar Dham Mandir Shivdansinghpura me sthit ek prasiddh dharmik sthal hai, jo Baba Chetan Das Ji ko samarpit hai.
Ye mandir lagbhag 700 saal purana mana jata hai aur Aravali pahadiyon ke beech sthit hai.

Baba Chetan Das Ji ki Tapasthali

Manyata hai ki Baba Chetan Das Ji ne isi sthan par kathor tapasya ki thi aur ant me Sahastris Pahadi me samadhi le gaye the.
Tapasthali tak pahunchne ke liye shraddhaluon ko lagbhag 260 seedhiyan chadhni padti hain.

Varsha Ritu ka Prakritik Jharna

Baarish ke mausam me Nidar Dham ke paas prakritik jharna behne lagta hai.
Is jharne ka aanand lene ke liye Delhi aur anya rajyon se bhi paryatak yahan aate hain.

Samajik Ekta aur Gaav ka Sahyog

Shivdansinghpura ki sabse badi taqat yahan ke logon ki samajik ekta hai. Dharmik aayojan, mela ya samajik kaam — har kaam me poora gaav ek saath khada hota hai, isi wajah se ye gaav “Nidar” kehlata hai.

Nishkarsh

Shivdansinghpura (Nidar Dham) ek aisa gaav hai jahan aastha, sanskriti, shiksha aur prakriti ka sundar sangam dekhne ko milta hai.
Sai Mata ka mela, Baba Chetan Das Ji ka mandir, shikshit yuva aur gaav ki ekta — ye sab milkar isse Rajasthan ke vishesh gaavon me shamil kar dete hain.
